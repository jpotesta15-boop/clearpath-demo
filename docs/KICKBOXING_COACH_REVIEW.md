# Kickboxing Coach Perspective: What Works and What Would Help

*Written as if by a coach who does private kickboxing lessons and is evaluating this app for day-to-day use.*

---

## What I Love

- **Client list and detail.** I can see everyone in one place, click into a client, and get contact info, stats (sessions completed, upcoming, last active), and notes. The notes are editable right there—huge for remembering who needs work on combos vs. conditioning.

- **Message button from client view.** One click to open messages with that client. No hunting for the thread.

- **Schedule view.** Month calendar with my availability and who’s booked. I click a client (from the available fighters list), choose date and time, and submit—the session is added to my calendar.

- **Programs and videos.** I can build a program from my video library (ordered lessons) and assign the whole thing to a client. Great for “here’s your 4-week routine” instead of sending one video at a time.

- **Dashboard at a glance.** Totals for clients, upcoming sessions, pending requests, revenue (and revenue this week in the demo), and unread messages. The week mini-calendar and expandable sections keep it scannable.

- **Single place for my business.** Clients, schedule, programs, videos, and messages in one app. No switching between notes, calendar, and DMs.

---

## What I Wish Could Be There

### Scheduling and availability

- **Client-requested times.** Let a client say “I’m free Tue/Thu 5–7pm” and show me those requests so I can confirm or suggest another time. Right now I’m the one proposing slots.

- **Two-way confirmations.** When I propose a session, the client sees it and can approve or reject. When a client requests a time, I can approve or reject. Both of us see the same “pending” and “confirmed” state.

- **Reminders.** Optional email or in-app reminder a day (or a few hours) before a session so no one forgets.

### Payments and money

- **Tracking who paid.** Even something simple: mark a session as “paid” or link to a payment (Stripe, Venmo, etc.) so I know who’s paid for the month.

- **Invoices or payment requests.** Generate a simple “you owe $X for these sessions” or send a payment link. Right now revenue is a number; I’d want it tied to actual payments per client.

### Lessons and content

- **Upload and share is already there.** Video library + assign to client (and programs) covers “upload and share lessons” well. The only thing I’d add is maybe “client watched this video” or “completed this lesson” so I know they’re following the program.

### Group and community

- **Group chats.** For clients who want group sessions (e.g. Saturday sparring), a group thread would help coordinate time and chat without mixing with 1:1 messages.

- **Group sessions.** Ability to create one session that multiple clients can book into (or that’s linked to a group), so the calendar shows “Group – 10am” and I see who’s in.

---

## Summary

The app already covers the core loop: clients, notes, schedule (click a client, choose date and time, add to calendar), programs as video routines, and messaging. Simple **session payment tracking** (“Mark as paid”) is in place. To go further, the next priorities would be: **client-requested times and two-way approve/reject**, **invoicing or payment links**, and **group chats / group sessions** for people who want to train together.

---

## Roadmap (deferred for later)

Planned for future releases so the demo stays simple:

- **Client-requested times** — Client submits preferred times; coach confirms or suggests another.
- **Two-way confirmations** — Client sees proposed sessions and can approve/reject; same “pending” / “confirmed” for both.
- **Reminders** — Email or in-app reminder before a session.
- **Invoices / payment requests** — “You owe $X for these sessions” or payment link (e.g. Stripe).
- **Video completion** — “Client watched this” or “completed this lesson” for program follow-through.
- **Group chats and group sessions** — Group thread and calendar entries for multiple clients (e.g. Saturday sparring).
